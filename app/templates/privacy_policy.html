Privacy Policy

To use the {{ app_name }} app, users must provide credentials (username/password) to be validated by the remote service.
Upon successful login a unique authentication token is generated and stored both in the app and in the remote service. This token is used to authenticate all subsequent requests. It expires after 30 days.

Besides the user credentials, no other personal data is collected or otherwise processed by the app.

Communication between the app and the remote service uses encrypted connections (HTTPS).
